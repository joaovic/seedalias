Seed alias generator

v.0.6.0

Use this web page to create an alias for your seed. Define a passphrase which you are familiar and can always remember. The passphrase will be used to encrypt and decrypt your seed. Optionally, you can scramble your encrypted seed to get higher protection.

After seed encryption, you should save the encrypted seed to a file and store in a secured environment. Never store it online or send it over wire.

If you are planning save it online or send it over wire, it is recommend that you scramble it first before saving it to a file. Scramble your encrypted seed providing a pair of bytes position for the byte swap operation.
After scramble your encrypted seed and save it to a file you may compress it with a password generating a binary file, achieving higher protection.

Example of scramble codes:

  • 0102, will swap the bytes at position 1 and 2, i.e., will swap the first by the second byte
  • 2107, will swap the bytes at positions 21 and 7
  • 21071312, will swap the bytes at positions 21 and 7. And after will swap bytes at positions 13 and 12
OBS: Positions are not zero based like array index. They are one (1) based.
OBS: You can provide as many position pairs as you want.
OBS: Position value less than 10 must be prefixed with digit zero (0). Ex: 09.
OBS: Positions must not be greater than encrypted seed bytes length.

OBS: You should keep the passphrase secret, it's recommended that you do not write it down. It should be a sentence that you will never forget, so keep in your mind.
